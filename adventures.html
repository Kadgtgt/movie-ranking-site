Find the best Adventure Movies here.

Dugeons and Dragons

A bard, a barbarian, a sorcerer, and a druid, each with their unique skills and personalities, they journey through a fantastical world filled with magic, monsters, and treacherous landscapes, they must overcome various challenges and confront dark forces that threaten their world.

Jungle Cruise

Dr. Lily Houghton (Blunt), a scientist seeking the legendary Tears of the Moon tree in the Amazon for its healing properties. She hires riverboat captain Frank Wolff (Johnson) to navigate the dangerous river. Together, they face wild animals, supernatural forces, and the villainous Prince Joachim (Jesse Plemons).

Damsel

In "Damsel," Millie Bobby Brown portrays a young woman who is seemingly promised in marriage to a handsome prince. However, her fairy tale quickly unravels when she discovers that she is not destined to live a life of royalty but is instead being prepared as a sacrificial offering to a deadly dragon.

TinTin

Tintin and his loyal dog, as they discover a model ship containing a clue about a long-lost treasure. Their investigation leads them to meet the cantankerous Captain Haddock who is connected to the treasure. They embark on a thrilling global adventure to find the hidden fortune.

Jumanji

Four high school students are transported into the world of Jumanji after discovering a video game version of the board game. They become their chosen avatars: Dr. Smolder Bravestone, Franklin "Mouse" Finbar, Professor Shelly Oberon, and Ruby Roundhouse. To return to the real world, they must work together to complete the game and survive its various challenges.

Lord of the Rings

Set in the mythical world of Middle-earth, the story follows a young hobbit named Frodo Baggins, who is entrusted with an ancient and powerful artifact known as the One Ring. The ring was created by the dark lord Sauron to control all other rings of power and conquer Middle-earth.
