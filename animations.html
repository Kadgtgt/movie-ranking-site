Kungu-Fu Panda

Po, who works in his family's noodle shop but aspires to become a kung fu warrior. His life takes an unexpected turn when he is unexpectedly chosen as the Dragon Warrior, a legendary fighter destined to bring peace to the valley.

Blue Eye Samurai

Set in Edo-period Japan, the story follows a mixed-race samurai on a quest for revenge, grappling with identity and belonging in a society that shuns them for their blue eyes.

Attack On Titan

Eren Yeager, his adoptive sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their friend Armin Arlert. Their lives change forever when a colossal Titan breaches the outermost wall, leading to the destruction of their hometown and the death of Eren’s mother. This traumatic event drives Eren to vow revenge and to join the military to fight the Titans.

Moana

Chosen by the ocean itself, Moana sets out on a daring journey to save her people when a terrible curse incurred by the demigod Maui reaches her island. Moana must find Maui, who stole the heart of the goddess Te Fiti, and return it to save her island and restore balance.

Raya and The last Dragon

A young warrior named Raya. Long ago, humans and dragons lived together harmoniously in Kumandra. However, a sinister force known as the Druun threatened the land, turning people and dragons to stone. The dragons sacrificed themselves to save humanity, leaving behind a magical gem to protect the land.

Super Mario Bros

Mario and Luigi, two Brooklyn plumbers, stumble upon this alternate dimension while trying to rescue Daisy, a young woman who turns out to be a princess from this world. King Koopa, the tyrannical ruler of Dinohattan, has kidnapped Daisy to merge the human and dinosaur worlds, allowing him to take over.
