Movies

The Bee Keeper

"The Beekeeper" combines intense action sequences with a gripping storyline, showcasing Statham's signature tough-guy persona. The film explores themes of revenge, justice, and the consequences of living a life shrouded in secrecy.

Jungle Cruise

Dr. Lily Houghton (Blunt), a scientist seeking the legendary Tears of the Moon tree in the Amazon for its healing properties. She hires riverboat captain Frank Wolff (Johnson) to navigate the dangerous river. Together, they face wild animals, supernatural forces, and the villainous Prince Joachim (Jesse Plemons).

Kung-fu Panda

Set in ancient China, the story follows Po, who works in his family's noodle shop but aspires to become a kung fu warrior. His life takes an unexpected turn when he is unexpectedly chosen as the Dragon Warrior, a legendary fighter destined to bring peace to the valley.

Boy Kills World

The movie stars Bill Skarsgård as Boy, a deaf-mute with a vivid imagination who escapes to a primal jungle after his family is murdered. Under the mentorship of a shaman, Boy transforms into an adept warrior, fueled by a desire for revenge.

Damsel

In "Damsel," Millie Bobby Brown portrays a young woman who is seemingly promised in marriage to a handsome prince. However, her fairy tale quickly unravels when she discovers that she is not destined to live a life of royalty but is instead being prepared as a sacrificial offering to a deadly dragon.

Blue Eye Samurai

Set in Edo-period Japan, the story follows a mixed-race samurai on a quest for revenge, grappling with identity and belonging in a society that shuns them for their blue eyes.
